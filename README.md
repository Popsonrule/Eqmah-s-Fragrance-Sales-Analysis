# Eqmah-s-Fragrance-Sales-Analysis
This is an analysis of a retail perfume business that goes by the name "Eqmah's Fragrance". 
The objectives of this sales analysis are;
To determine the total capital and profit generated from July to December.
To determine the total capital and profit generated each month and also the percentage increase in the the total capital month over month.
To examine the product and category performance of the perfumes sold within the period.
To determine the total profit generated from each perfume product and category throughout the period.
